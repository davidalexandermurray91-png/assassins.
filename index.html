ASSASSINS

🎯 Goal

Survive the night in the hotel. Earn money during the day, buy weapons, then survive 90 seconds of assassins each night.

⚔ Combat

ATK – Attack with sword (melee) or gun (ranged, any direction).

JUMP – Jump attack. Landing near assassins = 2× damage with sword.

🌙 Night Survival

HIDE – Near a dark closet: hide for 12 s. Companion distracts assassins.

SPRINT ⚠ – Alerts ALL nearby assassins! Avoid unless desperate.

☀ Day Activities

USE – Near a green worker (NPC): earn $10.

BUY – Near a shop counter: ⚔ Sword $30 (melee) · 🔫 Gun $50 (ranged).

👁 Fog of War

Limited vision at night — assassins may lurk just around the corner!

💾 Account

Press ESC on title to log in / create an account and save progress.
