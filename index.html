ASSASSINS

🎯 Goal

Survive nights in the hotel. Earn money by day, buy weapons, fight or hide at night.

⚔ Combat

Left-click / ATK — Sword melee (range ~1.5 m) or Gun raycast (gun has priority).

☾ Night Survival

H — Near a dark closet: hide for 12 s. Companion distracts assassins.

Shift ⚠ — Sprint alerts ALL nearby assassins. Use as a last resort only.

☀ Day Activities

E — Near a green NPC worker: earn $10.

C — Near a shop counter: ⚔ Sword $30 · 🔫 Gun $50.

🙃 Controls

WASD: move · Mouse: look (click game first to lock cursor)

Left-click: attack · E: work · C: buy · H: hide · Shift: sprint · ESC: unlock mouse

💾 Account

Press ESC on the title screen to log in / create an account and save progress.
