ASSASSINS
Hotel Survival — First Person Edition

🎯 Goal

Survive in a hotel. Earn money each day, buy weapons, survive assassins each night.

☀ Day (60s) — E: work   C: buy   R: sleep in bed

Each job earns $10. Sword $30 (melee). Gun $50 (raycast). Sleep restores 1 HP.

☾ Night (90s) — Left-click: attack   H: hide

Hide in wardrobes or under beds for 12s. Sprint (Shift) alerts ALL assassins!

Controls

WASD: move · Mouse: look · H: hide · R: sleep · Shift: sprint · Stairs: auto
